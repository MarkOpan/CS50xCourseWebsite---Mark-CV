Product Market Fat

⭐ Product Market Fat is a Ukrainian-language podcast about product management, product IT, and the work of a product manager. We are present on major podcast platforms and social media. The podcast was created by two product managers - Mark and Ivan, who work on different products and in different companies, but they are united by product thinking and interest in their profession.

Each new episode is a different story. We talk to different product managers from different companies about different product and non-product topics, as well as share our own experience and advice in product management.

Enjoy listening to us!
